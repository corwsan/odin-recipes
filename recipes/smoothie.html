Smoothie

Smoothies are great and tasty and healthy and easy and customizable

Ingredients

  • Coconut Milk
  • Coconut Cream
  • Honey
  • Ice
  • Fruit of your choosing

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend until everything is combined
  3. Pour into your favorite cup
  4. Drink up
  5. Be refreshed
