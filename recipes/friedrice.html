Fried Rice

Chiense style fried rice with bacon and chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bacon
  • Rice
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions

  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Rice Vinegar

Steps

  1. Chop onions and fry in oil
  2. Add bacon and fry until golden brown
  3. Add diced bell peppers
  4. Add diced chicken
  5. Add cooked rice

  6. Combine sauces and vinegar in small bowl
  7. Pour over fried rice
  8. Mix well and let simmer until all sauce is evaporated
